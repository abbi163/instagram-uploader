Did you know? She was officially recognized by the Guinness World Records as the youngest solo artist in the world to release a song album.

Nonoka Murakata (村方乃々佳), also popularly known as Nono-chan, is a Japanese child prodigy and singer.

She composed and released her debut solo album, titled Nono-chan 2 Years Old Children's Songs, at the age of 2.
